GK Brand

The following fonts, colors & logos are used throughout the Genius Kitchen website.

Brand Fonts

Brandon Grotesque

Brandon Grotesque is used for most headlines, as well as button text. Brandon Grotesque should always be used UPPERCASE, and with 1px kerning.

Gotham

Gotham is the default font for body copy. It may sometimes be used UPPERCASE, but generally should be considered a mixed-case font.

Favicon & Touch Icons

Favicons and Touch Icons are used when a user bookmarks or adds the Genius Kitchen site to a device home screen, respectively.

Social Sharing Graphics

Since some recipes do not have photos, for social sites such as Facebook & Pinterest we need to have a default image to use in its place.

Default Avatars

In order to ensure all users have a photo and to ensure variety, there is a set of eight default avatars, assigned at random during registration. Users are then free to either choose another from the set or upload their own.
